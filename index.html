To play this game all you have to do is aim your arrow, set your strength, and shoot!
To line up your shot click the bottom arrows. Make sure to pay attention to the wind factor before you hit.
Time when you press hit so that you get the strength you want. Scoring is based on how close to the pin you land.  Here is a practice hole. Good Luck!
